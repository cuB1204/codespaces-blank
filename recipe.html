Shrimp Rasta Pasta

This recipe consists of stir-fried shrimp with sautaed green and red bell peppers with a cheesy sauce and your choice of pasta noodle. This recipe holds a special place in my heart as my signature whenever my family asks me to cook they usually ask me to make a rendition of this recipe and this is the one of the only things I can say I've truly mastered making and I no longer require a recipe book to make.

Ingredients

  • 1 Bag of Uncooked Shrimp Tail On or Off
  • 1 16 Oz Box of Your Choice Pasta
  • 1 Whole Red Bell Pepper
  • 1 Whole Green Bell Pepper
  • Can of Evaporated Milk
  • 2 Cups of Shredded Cheese

Instructions

  1. Put water in a pot to boil then put your pasta in until cooked then strain of the water and set pasta to the side
  2. Grab your bell peppers then cut them into half inch long strips and remove the core
  3. Season your Shrimp
  4. Put oil in a pan and start to sautaue the bell peppers
  5. When peppers are halfway cooked add shrimp and stir-fry the shrimp
  6. After shrimp is around 3/4 cooked open your can of Evaporated milk and pour the whole thing in
  7. Then put the 2 cups of shredded cheese in the pan and melt cheese
  8. Once Cheese is melted keep pan on low heat until sauce is thick
  9. Add pasta into pan mix thoroughly and enjoy!
